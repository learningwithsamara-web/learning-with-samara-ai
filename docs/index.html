🔒 Privacy Policy

🔒
Your Privacy Matters to Us
Learning With Samara AI™ is committed to protecting the privacy and safety of every child and family on our platform. This policy explains clearly what we collect, what we don't, and how we keep your data safe.
Last updated: March 2026 · Geor De Mer LLC
✅ The Short Version — What We Promise
✅ We never sell your data to anyone — ever
✅ We never show ads — Learning With Samara AI™ is and always will be completely ad-free
✅ Homework photos are deleted immediately after processing
✅ We collect only what we need to run the platform
✅ Children under 13 require parental consent to register
✅ You can delete your account and all data at any time
✅ Your payment info is handled securely by our payment processor — we never see your card details
1. Who We Are
Learning With Samara AI™ is an educational platform operated by Geor De Mer LLC, a registered business in the United States of America.

Contact: edu@learningwithsamara.com
Website: learningwithsamara.com
2. What Information We Collect
When you register: Your email address, name and selected age group. This is used to create your account and personalise your learning experience.

When you use the platform: Quiz scores, subjects studied and progress data. This is used to track your learning journey and unlock features.

When you use Homework Helper: You may upload a photo of homework. This image is sent to our AI service for analysis and is immediately discarded after processing. We do not store, view or share homework photos under any circumstances.

When you subscribe to Pro: Payment is processed securely by our payment provider. We receive only confirmation that payment was made — we never see, store or have access to your card number, billing address or any payment details.
3. What We Do NOT Collect
❌ We do not collect your location
❌ We do not collect your phone number
❌ We do not use advertising trackers or pixels
❌ We do not sell any data to third parties
❌ We do not store homework or uploaded images
❌ We do not record voice or audio
❌ We do not share data with schools, governments or advertisers
4. Children's Privacy (COPPA)
We take the safety of children extremely seriously. Our platform serves children from birth to age 17.

Children under 13: A parent or guardian must register the account. By registering, you confirm you are the parent or legal guardian and consent to your child using the platform.

AI interactions: All AI chat on our platform uses strict content guardrails. Samara AI is programmed to only discuss educational topics and will refuse any inappropriate requests.

Data minimisation: We collect the minimum information necessary. Children's quiz results and progress are stored securely and used only to improve their learning experience on our platform.

If you believe your child has provided personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately at edu@learningwithsamara.com and we will delete it promptly.
5. How We Use Your Information
✅ To create and manage your account
✅ To track learning progress and unlock achievements
✅ To personalise content to your age group
✅ To process your subscription (via our secure payment provider)
✅ To send important account emails (password reset, receipts)
✅ To improve the platform based on anonymised usage patterns
6. Third-Party Services We Use
Payment Processor — All subscription payments are processed securely. We never see or store your card details or billing information.

YouTube — Educational videos in Samara TV are served by YouTube. YouTube's privacy policy applies when you watch videos.
7. Your Rights
You have the right to:

✅ Access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you
✅ Correction — Ask us to correct inaccurate information
✅ Deletion — Request that we delete your account and all associated data
✅ Portability — Request your data in a readable format
✅ Objection — Object to how we use your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at edu@learningwithsamara.com. We will respond within 30 days.
8. Data Security
All data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and row-level security — ensuring each user can only ever access their own data. All connections use HTTPS encryption. We regularly review our security practices to keep your information safe.
9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes by email. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
Questions About Your Privacy?
We are always happy to help. Reach out anytime.
