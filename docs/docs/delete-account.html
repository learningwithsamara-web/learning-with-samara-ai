🗑️ Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go! To delete your Learning With Samara AI account and all associated data, follow these steps:

Option 1 — Email Us

Send an email to edu@learningwithsamara.com with the subject line "Delete My Account" and include your registered email address. We will delete your account and all data within 30 days.

Option 2 — In App

Log in to your account at ai.learningwithsamara.com → Go to Settings → Select "Delete Account"

What gets deleted?

  • ✅ Your account and login credentials
  • ✅ All child profiles
  • ✅ All quiz scores and progress data
  • ✅ All coins and rewards
  • ✅ All personal information

What is retained?

  • Anonymised usage statistics (no personal information attached)
  • Transaction records required by law (subscription payment history)
